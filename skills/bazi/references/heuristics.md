# 八字理法启发式裁决

## Fixed Decision Flow

```text
1. Validate complete JSON.
2. Read pillars, hidden stems, ten gods, month command, jieqi, five-element scores, relations, luck cycles, target years.
3. Establish month-command environment.
4. Decide climate condition: cold, heat, dryness, dampness, balance.
5. Decide momentum condition: strong, weak, follow, transform, special structure, partiality, flow, blockage.
6. Select one leading mode: 调候主导 / 气势主导 / 格局主导.
7. Determine disease and medicine: 用神, 喜神, 忌神, 仇神, 闲神.
8. Verify through dayun and target years.
9. Produce structured reasoning and mingshu.
```

## Leading Modes

### 调候主导

Trigger when seasonal climate prevents the chart from functioning: severe winter cold, summer heat, dry earth/fire, or damp cold. In this mode, climate remedy is a system condition, not an ordinary optional favorable god.

Weights:

```text
穷通宝鉴 40
滴天髓 30
子平真诠 20
渊海子平 / 三命通会 7
千里命稿 3
```

Rule: a pattern may be named, but its usable level is lowered if climate is unresolved.

### 气势主导

Trigger when global momentum is decisive: follow-strength, follow-weakness, transformation, exclusive prosperity, two-element structure, severe partiality, or one force clearly controls the chart.

Weights:

```text
滴天髓 40
子平真诠 25
穷通宝鉴 20
渊海子平 / 三命通会 10
千里命稿 5
```

Rule: do not force ordinary month-command pattern analysis when the entire chart already follows another momentum.

### 格局主导

Default when climate is not fatal, momentum is not extreme, and the month-command pattern is legible.

Weights:

```text
子平真诠 40
滴天髓 25
穷通宝鉴 20
渊海子平 / 三命通会 10
千里命稿 5
```

Rule: determine the pattern, useful god, auxiliary god, success, failure, rescue, clarity, and level through month-command structure.

## Judgment Strength

- **A级定断**: primary mode is clear, source lenses converge, and luck confirms the structure.
- **B级主断**: evidence is strong; secondary disagreement does not alter the main line.
- **C级条件断**: the conclusion depends on luck, revealed stems, roots, combinations, or clashes.
- **D级不裁决**: required input is missing or signals cancel each other too strongly.
